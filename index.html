About Me

I started my online career in a small, 4-man website development team. There I learned HTML, CSS, and a ton about building websites. I worked on dozens of projects, and my day-to-day work varied from coding to content writing, search engine optimization, email marketing, social media management, and search engine marketing.

After leaving Elemental Blend, I became the head of the search marketing department at SpaceCraft Inc. At SpaceCraft, I specialized in creating and running SEM campaigns for clients. I also managed affiliate programs, social media, email marketing, and SEO for SpaceCraft's marketing website. My other responsibilities included training new employees on the basics of SEO and how they are applied to client work.

I am now a SEM Manager at HawkSEM where I work to plan, create, and optimize ROI driven digital marketing campaigns for a variety of clients. I also plan and oversee the creation of landing pages, mostly built on WordPress.

In my spare time I improve my web development skills, including learning JavaScript, JQuery, SASS, and whichever framework is the flavor of the month.

Local Weather App

The Local Weather App is an Intermediate Front End Development project from freeCodeCamp.

It fulfills the following user stories:
1) I can see the weather in my current location.
2) I can see a different icon or background image (e.g. snowy mountain, hot desert) depending on the weather.
3) I can push a button to toggle between Fahrenheit and Celsius.

It uses the Open Weather API.
