About Me

I started my online career in a small, 4-man website development team. There I learned HTML, CSS, a little Daniel Cleveland started his career by building personal websites, and was hired early on by Elemental Blend. There Daniel worked on dozens of projects in disciplines such as HTML, CSS, content writing, search engine optimization, email marketing, social media management, and search engine marketing. After leaving Elemental Blend, Daniel became the head of the search marketing department at SpaceCraft Inc., where he works today. At SpaceCraft, Daniel specializes in creating and running SEM campaigns for clients. He also manages affiliate programs, social media, email marketing, and SEO for SpaceCraft.
